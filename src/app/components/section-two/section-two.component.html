Our Services

With offices in Toronto and Baghdad, we offer a comprehensive range of services at every stage of film creation in both Iraq and Canada. Whether you're envisioning a sprawling feature film or a snappy short, we are adept at guiding your cinematic journey through the enchanting landscapes of Canada and the echoes of Sumer and Babylon in Iraq. At Purattu, we extend a warm welcome to individual artists as well as established film production companies and studios. Our approach is personalised, adapting to the unique requirements of each project and the needs of content producers. From the inception of creative ideas in writing rooms to full production services, picture editing, and DCP. Majority of our projects are treaty and non-treaty international co-productions. We aid content producers in navigating the intricacies of Canadian tax incentives, as well as applications to the funding programs of the Canadian Heritage and a variety of funds in the MENA region.

Why Choose Purattu

Like the innovators of old who crafted the wheel and the written word, we approach filmmaking with a contagious inventiveness. We hold deep respect for every creative endeavour, transforming the filmmaking process into a joyous leap into the realms of storytelling, and treat every project team as a family.
